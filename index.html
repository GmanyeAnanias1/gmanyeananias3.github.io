Ghana's Biggest Technical University

The Accra Technical University was established
in 1949 as a Technical School in Ghana and commissioned
in 1957 as Accra Technical Institute before being converted
into a Polytechnic in 2007 by the Parliament of Ghana.
It was later given a university status, becoming Accra Technical University in 2016.
